Welcome to PremoFM

PremoFM is a podcast app designed for those who love listening to podcasts. You'll be able to subscribe to hundreds of thousands of audio podcasts and listen to millions of podcast episodes, including the insanely popular ones like Serial, This American Life, and Stuff You Should Know.

PremoFM has been thoughtfully designed to follow Material Design while also adding unique flavor and interaction. Podcast episode show notes are displayed right up front, so no more digging through an app to find out what an episode is really about. The Now Playing experience is simple and unique, allowing quick and convenient control of your podcast playback.

Best of all, PremoFM is completely free and open source with no obstructing ads or tricky trials. PremoFM is listener-supported empowering podcast lovers to optionally contribute to support the continued development of PremoFM.
